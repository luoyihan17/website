Supported by the Shanghai government, 2021.03 - 2021.06. I founded and curated an exhibition of art and design student work across 11 universities in the U.S. and U.K.

The project collected 500+ submissions and selected 100+ works for exhibition. It invited 50+ industry alumni and 100 onsite students for exchange, with roughly 500 visitors per day. The exhibition was held at Shanghai Xintai Warehouse, a protected historical building with a project area of about 6,000 square meters.
