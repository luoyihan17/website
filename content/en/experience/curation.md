Curator & Designer, supported by the Yizhuang government, 2025.05 - 2025.09. Responsible for curatorial positioning, theme direction, exhibition structure, artist and team selection, international exchange platform, venue planning, installation flow, and audience experience.

## OMNI Art Expo

Curator & Founder, supported by the Shanghai government, 2021.03 - 2021.06. Organized students from 11 universities in the U.S. and U.K., collected 500+ submissions, selected 100+ exhibited works, and hosted exchanges with 50+ alumni and 100 onsite students, with roughly 500 visitors per day.
