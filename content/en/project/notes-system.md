World In A Cell is a VR educational game developed at USC World Building Media Lab to help students understand complex cell biology concepts.

As the VR UX Designer, I led VR interaction flow and UI design and conducted 30+ usability and scenario tests.

Outcomes:

- Improved immersion and learning efficiency;
- Solved operation and comprehension pain points through usability testing;
- Collaborated with biological scientists to develop a VR learning tool for classroom use;
- Introduced the tool into USC biology courses in Fall 2023.
