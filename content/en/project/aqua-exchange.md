## Vision

The derivatives market is massive, yet on-chain trading remains inaccessible to most people due to complex workflows, intimidating interfaces, and steep learning curves. AQUA was created to bridge this gap — making on-chain perpetual trading **easy to start** and **easy to use** for everyone.

## What We Offer

AQUA supports full on-chain derivatives trading across Hyperliquid/HIP-3 — crypto, tokenized stocks, metals, gaming assets, and more — with all the essential exchange capabilities including real-time charts, order management, position tracking, and market data. Users can follow and copy celebrity traders or AI agent strategies with one tap, access Hyperliquid Vaults for managed trading strategies, and earn rewards through a built-in referral system with points and commission rebates. The mobile-first design features fiat on-ramps and a social-login, no-mnemonic wallet for secure, low-friction trading.
