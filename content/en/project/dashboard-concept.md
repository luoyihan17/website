Vemus is an independent AI singing and music creation app exploring user-facing AI-generated music experiences.

I participated in building the product from 0 to 1, focusing on core experience design, creation flow definition, and interaction delivery. The product is planned for launch in late 2025 with a target of 100k+ DAU.

Design focus:

- Lowering the barrier to AI singing and songwriting;
- Helping users understand generation progress and results;
- Creating a smooth loop across creation, previewing, adjustment, and publishing;
- Preparing product paths for growth and community sharing.
